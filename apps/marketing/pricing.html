FAQ

Pricing questions, answered.

What happens if I run out of credits?

The studio stays open. You can still iterate, browse, and export — you just can't generate something new until the next cycle, or until you upgrade. We never lock you out of your work.

Can I switch plans mid-month?

Yes. Upgrades are immediate and prorated. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.

Do credits roll over?

Monthly credits reset each cycle. We've sized the plans for steady weekly throughput, not stockpiling. If you regularly hit the cap, you're on the wrong plan — talk to us.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every new workspace starts with 100 credits, no card required. That's enough to import a website, generate ICPs and produce a handful of full creatives.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes. Annual billing is available on Starter and Pro and discounts the equivalent of two months. Available at checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards via Stripe. Enterprise plans support invoicing and bank transfer.
