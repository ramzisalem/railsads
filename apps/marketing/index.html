FAQ

Everything you might be wondering.

Is RailsAds another AI ad generator?

No. RailsAds is a workspace. Each brand has products, ICPs, competitors and threads. The AI doesn't start from a blank prompt — it generates from a composed context. The result is creative that's grounded, not generic.

What counts as a "creative"?

A full creative — hooks, primary text, headlines, optional visual prompt — costs 15 credits. Generating an image costs 25 credits. ICP generation: 5 credits. Competitor analysis: 10. Refining what's already on screen costs nothing.

Do I need to bring my own ad data?

No. You only need a website. We extract brand identity and products automatically. Adding competitor screenshots is optional — but it's where most of the strategic edge comes from.

Can I edit a creative I made yesterday?

Yes. Every creative lives in a thread tied to its product, ICP, template and angle. Reopen the thread and the studio restores everything — context panel, history, and current creative state.

What does a free trial give me?

100 credits, no card required. That's enough to import a website, generate ICPs, and produce a handful of full creatives. After that, choose a plan.

Is my data safe?

Your brand data, products, threads and creatives stay in your workspace. We never train models on your content. Enterprise plans include SOC2-aligned controls and a DPA.
