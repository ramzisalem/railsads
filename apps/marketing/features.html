Features

The whole creative system,
not a prompt box.

Six pieces, all stitched together. Each one carries context to the next, so the system always knows what you're working on, who it's for, and how you want it to sound.

01 · Brand

Your brand DNA, captured automatically.

Paste a URL. We extract positioning, tone of voice, palette, personality and products. Review inline, edit anything. The result is the foundation every creative is built from.

Positioning, value props and tone, ready to edit.

Palette and style tags pulled from your live site.

Re-import any time to stay in sync with your site.

02 · Products

Products are the operational hub.

Real workflows start from a product. Each product owns its ICPs, competitor signals and creative threads. Click "Create ad" and the studio opens with everything pre-loaded.

Imported automatically from your site, edit anything inline.

One-click jump to studio with product, ICP and template ready.

Product-level history of every creative ever generated.

03 · ICPs

Audience profiles that actually shape output.

Each ICP has pains, desires, objections and triggers. Switch ICPs and the same product produces a completely different creative — same brand, different lens.

Generated from product context — not generic personas.

Multiple ICPs per product, each independently addressable.

Every ICP carries directly into creative generation.

ICP — Busy professionals

Time-poor, performance-driven, want healthy without the fuss.

Pains

Skipping breakfast, decision fatigue, expensive smoothie shops.

Desires

Energy without effort. Predictable nutrition. Less waste.

Triggers

Monday mornings. Gym days. Travel weeks.

Hook patterns

"Stop doing X" appears in 6/9 ads. Pain-first dominates.

Visual patterns

Before / after frames, kitchen-counter contexts, warm tones.

Emotional drivers

Convenience > status > nostalgia. Convenience wins by 2x.

04 · Competitors

Competitor intelligence, mapped to action.

Drop screenshots or paste links. We extract recurring hooks, angles, emotional triggers, visuals and offers — and map them to your products so the patterns shape your next creative, not just a report.

Manual or paste-link competitor entries — you stay in control.

Patterns mapped to specific products in your workspace.

Insights flow into the studio as optional creative ammunition.

05 · Creative Studio

One chat box.
Structured outputs.
Persistent threads.

The studio is where creative actually happens. The right panel always shows context: product, ICP, template, angle, awareness. The bottom is one input. Outputs render as blocks you can use, improve or copy.
